About the Event

Marine Traffic, the flagship event of AMET University, Chennai, is a dynamic conclave that unites maritime students, industry leaders, and professionals for an unparalleled experience of learning, competition, and networking. Featuring summits, debates, technical challenges, sports events, and inter-university competitions, this event bridges the gap between academia and industry, offering students hands-on training through simulations, maritime drills, and expert-led workshops. With top recruiters and corporate leaders in attendance, Marine Traffic provides a gateway to career and internship opportunities, fostering leadership, teamwork, and innovation. Rooted in a vision to create a global hub for maritime excellence, the event promotes sustainability, automation, and advancements in marine safety, shaping the future of the shipping industry. Hosted by AMET University, India’s first private maritime university, with state-of-the-art simulators, a fully equipped ship-in-campus, and global industry collaborations, Marine Traffic stands as a launchpad for aspiring maritime professionals. With three decades of excellence, AMET continues to produce world-class seafarers and industry-ready graduates, making Marine Traffic the ultimate celebration of maritime talent, leadership, and innovation.
